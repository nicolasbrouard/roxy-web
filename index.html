The Band

Roxy Thunder (Emmanuel Raymond, Kriss Ivanov, Javier Guevara Garcia and Carmelo Fuizzotto) is a fresh, high-energy rock band rising from Montréal's vibrant scene.

Founded in 2025, they blend classic-rock power with modern attitude, delivering big riffs, catchy hooks, and electrifying presence.

In March 2026, they released their debut album, instantly marking their arrival as a rising force to watch. Loud, dynamic, and built for the stage, Roxy Thunder brings raw heart and unstoppable fire—a new force ready to shake the city and spark the next rock anthem.
